The PubNub Transportation Solution Kit

The PubNub Fleet Management Solution Kit provides the real-time building blocks to jump start the development of a transportation management application, including taxi, fleet management and delivery service apps. Built on the PubNub Real-Time Network, the solution kit components are supported on over 50+ SDKs and are globally-scaled, reliable and secure.

Location Tracking

Stream location data from each vehicle and see a real-time map view of all vehicle locations. Understand delivery or arrival times to provide better customer experience.

Dispatch

Respond to transportation demand in real-time. Manage real-time messaging and response between vehicles and dispatch, regardless of network conditions.

Vehicle Status

See a real-time view of your fleet status and availability. Detect when vehicles are available, booked, fully-loaded or other status.
